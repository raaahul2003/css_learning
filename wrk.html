KOZHIKODE



Kozhikode is located along the Malabar Coast in Kerala and is the second largest city in the state, with a population of over 600,000 within its municipal boundaries . Historically, it was the capital of the Zamorin kingdom and later an important trading hub for Chinese, Arab, Persian, and European merchants . The city played a key role in maritime trade, and the word "calico," referring to fine cotton fabric, is derived from Calicut . In 2023, Kozhikode was recognized by UNESCO as India’s first City of Literature
